The Mercedes AMG One is a hypercar that brings Formula 1 technology to the road. It features a 1.6-liter V6 engine paired with a hybrid system, producing a total output of 1,063 horsepower. This car is designed for extreme performance, achieving 0-100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds and a top speed of 352 km/h. The AMG One is equipped with an 8.4 kWh battery, allowing for impressive electric power delivery of 483 hp. In terms of design, the AMG One is characterized by its aerodynamic shape and aggressive styling, reminiscent of F1 cars. It is available in a variety of striking colors, including Iridium Silver, Designo Graphite Grey, and Designo Manufaktur Patagonia Red, among others, allowing owners to customize their vehicles to their preferences.
